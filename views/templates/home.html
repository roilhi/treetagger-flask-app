LCR Lab: Learner Corpus Research Laboratory

LCR Lab is an independent research initiative dedicated to the design, development, and analysis of learner corpora and linguistically informed digital tools. The laboratory integrates corpus compilation, a morphological database construction for an NLP tool and a seminar series devoted to disseminate Learner Corpus Research methodologies and applications.

The laboratory currently hosts three core developments:

  • MexLeC 3.0, a longitudinal Mexican Learner Corpus
  • Morph 1.0, a morphological analysis tool and database
  • Seminario Permanente de LCR, community
